CORE PRINCIPLES

These are the foundational principles of the server's community:

Bitcoin Paid Whitelist

In an attempt to build conscious and ethic community, we require a fee to be paid in bitcoin to join the server.

Consider it as a social experiment - can bitcoin be used to filter out bad actors, commonly referred to as griefers?

The fee will go towards server maintenance and development.

Vanilla Setup

We see value in not chasing the most modern experience, but rather going deeper in exploration of the original and time tested setup.

Full Transparency

Logs are publicly available, making possible an investigation of any incident.

Server backups are available for download publicly, eliminating the risk of server going permanently down.

Financial records are public, making possible to track the flow of entry fees and donations.
